Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Order Cancellation

Pre-Dispatch Cancellation: You may cancel an order for a full refund at any point before it is dispatched for delivery.
Post-Dispatch Cancellation: Due to the logistics involved with construction materials, cancellations are not possible once the order has been dispatched.

2. Returns and Refunds

Inspection on Delivery: For items like rebar couplers, you must inspect the products at the time of delivery. Do not accept a damaged or incorrect shipment. Returns or replacements for such issues must be initiated on the spot.

Return Window (Smaller Items): For smaller construction materials, returns are accepted within 2 days of delivery, provided the items are unused, in their original packaging, and in returnable condition.

Non-Returnable Items: Custom-made products, cut-to-size materials, or items damaged during installation are not eligible for return.

Damaged/Defective Items: If you receive a defective or incorrect item, you must notify our customer service within 2 days with photographic evidence to arrange for a replacement or refund.
